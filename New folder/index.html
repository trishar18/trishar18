Company Brief

LuminaGear is a forward-thinking brand that specializes in creating high-quality outdoor adventure equipment for enthusiasts and explorers. With a focus on innovation, durability, and style, LuminaGear aims to enhance the outdoor experience for individuals who seek adventure and appreciate top-notch gear.

LuminaGear is known for its sleek and futuristic design aesthetic. The products feature clean lines, bold colors, and an emphasis on functionality. The brand's logo should showcases a stylized mountain peak, symbolizing adventure and exploration. The overall design language should aligns with the brand's commitment to innovation and modernity.


Idea behind the logo
The primary concept was to create mountains using the letters L and G, which are the initials of the company name. However, as this is a trekking firm, I had to include something linked to trekking, so if you look closely, you can see a person climbing with the aid of a stick. which also resembles a mountain, and the initials L and G are visible when the logo is turned upside down. Since lumina also means "sun," I also wanted to include the sun in the logo.
Colours

why colour brown and orange

Earthiness, Dark brown is often associated with the earth, soil, and natural elements. Simplicity, Dark brown can also be seen as a color of simplicity, warmth, and comfort. Stability and Reliability, Brown is seen as a stable and reliable color. It can convey a sense of security, dependability, and resilience.

Energy and enthusiasm, Orange is commonly associated with energy, enthusiasm, and excitement. Creativity, It can also symbolize creativity, originality, and a sense of adventure. Vitality and warmth, Orange often evokes feelings of vitality, warmth, and positivity. Social interaction, It is a vibrant color that can be linked to socialization and extroversion.
